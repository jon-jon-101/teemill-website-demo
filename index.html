Sustainability

Better for business. Better for the planet.

Sustainable isn't a badge — it's built into how Teemill works. Every order is a step toward a circular economy.

🌿

Organic materials

Every product is made from natural, certified organic materials. No synthetic fibres, no harmful dyes.

☀️

Renewable energy

Our factory runs on 100% renewable energy. Every item printed has a carbon footprint we actively track and reduce.

♻️

Circular design

Products are designed to be sent back and remade. We run a take-back scheme so nothing ends up in landfill.

📦

Plastic-free packaging

Every order ships in 100% plastic-free packaging. No poly bags, no bubble wrap, no waste.

🏭

UK-based production

Made in Britain, shipped fast. Shorter supply chains mean fewer emissions and faster delivery for your customers.

Independently certified and recognised for sustainability leadership across the industry. Not greenwashing — proof.
